LumaBeatz LLC Privacy Policy

Effective Date:  May 8, 2025
Last Updated: May 8, 2025

1. Who We Are
LumaBeatz LLC provides professional DJ, photo, video, and photobooth services for weddings, corporate functions, school events, and other special occasions.

2. Information We Collect
We collect personal information when you:

  • Submit a booking request
  • Fill out an inquiry form
  • Subscribe to updates or promotions
  • Communicate with us via phone, SMS, or email

This may include:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Event details (date, venue, preferences)
  • Payment-related information (where applicable)

3. How We Use Your Information
We use your information for the following purposes:
Business Communication

  • Sending proposals, contracts, and invoices
  • Confirming or scheduling meetings
  • Sending payment reminders or service updates

Marketing Communication

  • Sharing promotions, event highlights, or seasonal offers
  • Sending follow-up messages after inquiries
  • Providing links to our website or social media platforms

We will only send SMS messages to individuals who have provided explicit consent to receive them.

4. How We Share Information
We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing or commercial purposes. We are committed to maintaining your privacy and only use your data to provide and improve our services.
We may share limited data only with trusted third-party service providers necessary to operate our business, such as:

  • Payment processors or scheduling tools we use
  • SMS and email service providers (to deliver messages you opted in to receive)
  • Legal authorities, if required by law

We do not share your phone number or SMS opt-in consent information with third parties for their own marketing or independent use. This information is used solely for communication directly related to LumaBeatz LLC’s services.

5. SMS/Text Messaging
By providing your phone number and opting in, you agree to receive text messages from LumaBeatz LLC related to:

  • Your event planning
  • Service updates or confirmations
  • Marketing content or promotions

Message frequency varies. Standard message and data rates may apply.
You can opt out anytime by replying STOP to any of our messages.

6. Your Privacy Rights
You may request to:

  • Access or update your personal data
  • Opt out of SMS or email communication
  • Request deletion of your data

To make a request, email us at: info@lumabeatz.com

7. Security
We take reasonable precautions to protect your information using secure systems and access controls.
